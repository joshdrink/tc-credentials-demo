My Skills

Here you will find a list of your skills, separated into 2 main groups: claimed skills and verified skills. These skills define your areas of expertise using evidence that you supply by relating skills to your past and current experiences. You can add a skill to your profile using the "Add a Skill" button above.

My Claimed Skills

Claimed skills are the skills that you have that have not yet been verified through a standardized assessment. These skills offer initial insight into your abilities and how your experiences showcase your level of expertise.

My Verified Skills

Verified skills are skills that you have been assessed on during a government hiring process. Based on your results during the assessment, the manager decides to verify that you have the skill at the level you've claimed. Assessment allows these skills to offer more robust, verifiable evidence. Please note that some verified skills can expire over a period of time and will be changed from Verified to Expired if the expiration date is reached and a new assessment hasn't taken place.

You don't have any verified skills yet. Skills can be verified during an application process by a manager who has performed an assessment on your skill claims.
